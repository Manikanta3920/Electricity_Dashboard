SmartGrid Monitor 2019-2020

About the Project

This dashboard visualizes electricity consumption data across different states and regions of India for 2019 and 2020. Our aim is to make data more accessible and enable better understanding of energy trends and consumption behavior.

The project leverages Tableau for interactive visualizations, allowing you to filter and explore data easily. Built as part of an academic project to help policy makers, researchers, and students gain actionable insights into electricity usage.
